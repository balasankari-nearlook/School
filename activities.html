Acitivities

At Morning Dew Nursery and Primary School, we understand the importance of holistic development. Alongside academic excellence, we offer a range of extracurricular activities to foster physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

Karate

Discover the art of self-defense in our Karate sessions, where you'll learn essential techniques, build confidence, and stay active in a safe environment.

Yoga

Join our invigorating Yoga classes to strengthen your body, calm your mind, improve flexibility, and foster inner peace, promoting overall well-being and inner harmony.

Dance

Experience the joy of movement in our Dance sessions, where you'll unleash your creativity, express yourself, and build teamwork skills in a supportive atmosphere.

Drawing

Unleash your creativity with our Drawing sessions, where you'll learn basic techniques, explore various mediums, and express yourself artistically in a fun and nurturing environment.
